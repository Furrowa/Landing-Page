Best Smart Irrigation Solution for Small Farmers in India

May 1, 20265 min read

With erratic monsoons, depleting groundwater, and rising electricity costs, Indian farmers need smarter irrigation — but most "smart" solutions on the market are either too expensive or too complex for small and marginal farmers.

Oriva is different. It is a portable irrigation advisory system built specifically for the reality of Indian farming — affordable, simple, and completely offline.

How Oriva Works: 3 Simple Steps

  1. Insert the Oriva probe in the soil
    Place the probe in the root zone of your crop at the spot you want to measure.
  2. Connect to the app via Bluetooth
    Open the Oriva App on your smartphone. It connects instantly via Bluetooth — no internet, no SIM card needed.
  3. Get an instant recommendation
    The app shows you clearly: "Irrigate for 45 minutes today" — or tells you to wait. No interpretation needed.

What You Get

  • ✅ When to start watering — based on real soil moisture measurement
  • ✅ How long to water — precise duration, not a vague estimate
  • ✅ Works offline — Bluetooth only, no internet required in the field
  • ✅ Multiple languages — recommendations in your local language
  • ✅ Portable — carry it across different spots in your field for multiple readings

Ideal for All Irrigation Methods

Oriva works with drip irrigation, sprinkler systems, and flood irrigation. Whatever method you use, Oriva tells you when to run it and for how long — so every drop counts.

Why Small Farmers Love It

Oriva requires no installation, no connectivity infrastructure, and no ongoing costs. It is a one-time, affordable purchase that pays for itself quickly through reduced electricity and water waste. Farmers can measure multiple spots across their field in minutes and carry the device between fields.

Simple, reliable, and made for real Indian farmers.
