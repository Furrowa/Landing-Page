How It Works

From Ground to Phone
in 3 Simple Steps

  1. 1

    Plant the Device

    Place the sensor stake into your field. No wiring, no tools needed — solar panel charges within 2 hours of sunlight.

  2. 2

    Get a Clear Irrigation Decision

    Oriva continuously monitors soil moisture and analyzes conditions. You get simple alerts like "Irrigate today" or "Wait 3 days" — so you never overwater or underwater your crop.

  3. 3

    Act on Smart Recommendations

    Follow the alert, irrigate with confidence, and track your water use over the season — less waste, better yield, simpler farming.

  4. 4

    Watch Your Yield Grow

    Track your water use and field health season over season. Less overwatering, healthier crops, better outcomes — with data you can actually trust.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do Furrowa devices need a SIM card or internet connection?
No. Furrowa devices use LoRaWAN technology which doesn't require a SIM card. They transmit data up to 5 km to any Furrowa gateway. The gateway connects to the internet via a basic broadband or mobile connection shared at the village level.
How affordable are the devices? Are there subsidies available?
Oriva starts at ₹4,999 — a one-time purchase. The app is free forever. We are actively exploring financing and subsidy options as we grow.
In which languages does the Furrowa app work?
The Furrowa app supports 12 Indian languages: Hindi, English, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Gujarati, Punjabi, Odia, Malayalam, and Assamese. Alerts are sent via in-app notifications and WhatsApp in your preferred language.
Which states does Furrowa currently serve?
Oriva is currently in early deployment with pilot farmers. We are expanding our reach and would love to hear from farmers interested in trying it — reach out via our contact page.
How much can Furrowa realistically increase my crop yield?
Oriva helps you avoid overwatering and underwatering — two of the biggest causes of yield loss. By irrigating at the right time based on real soil data, farmers can reduce water waste and improve crop health. We are currently gathering data from our pilot deployments.
What warranty and after-sales support does Furrowa offer?
Oriva comes with a manufacturer warranty. We offer support directly — reach us via email or phone and our team will help you get sorted. We are a small, responsive team that takes support seriously.
