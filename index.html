How It Works

From Soil to Smart Decisions
in 4 Simple Steps

  1. 1

    Place Oriva in Your Field

    Insert the Oriva probe into the soil at root level. No wiring, complicated setup, or internet connection required.

  2. 2

    Take a Reading

    Oriva measures soil moisture, soil temperature, humidity, and field conditions directly from your crop zone.

  3. 3

    Get Irrigation Advice

    The app converts sensor data into clear recommendations like “Irrigate Today” or “Wait 3 Days” — simple decisions without guesswork.

  4. 4

    Save Water & Improve Yield

    Avoid overwatering, reduce unnecessary irrigation, and maintain healthier crop growth throughout the season.
