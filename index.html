How It Works

From Ground to Phone
in 3 Simple Steps

  1. 1

    Plant the Device

    Place the sensor stake into your field. No wiring, no tools needed — solar panel charges within 2 hours of sunlight.

  2. 2

    Get a Clear Irrigation Decision

    Oriva continuously monitors soil moisture and analyzes conditions. You get simple alerts like "Irrigate today" or "Wait 3 days" — so you never overwater or underwater your crop.

  3. 3

    Act on Smart Recommendations

    Follow the alert, irrigate with confidence, and track your water use over the season — less waste, better yield, simpler farming.

  4. 4

    Watch Your Yield Grow

    Track your water use and field health season over season. Less overwatering, healthier crops, better outcomes — with data you can actually trust.
